Smooth & Strong Shampoo

Smooth & Strong Shampoo is a cleansing product that targets the dual concerns of hair fragility and rough texture.

Benefits:

  • Reduces Hair Breakage: It is formulated with strengthening complexes that help reinforce weak hair bonds leading to a noticeable reduction in breakage and split ends over time.
  • Controls Frizz and Improves Texture: The smoothing agents gently coat the hair surface, which helps seal the cuticle, resulting in instantly smoother, less frizzy hair.
  • Enhances Manageability and Softness: Regular use makes the hair softer, easier to comb, and less prone to tangling, improving overall manageability.
  • Gentle and Nourishing Cleanse: It effectively cleanses the scalp and hair of dirt and buildup without over-drying, ensuring the hair retains essential moisture and natural shine.

Directions:

Wet your hair thoroughly, then apply a small amount of shampoo and gently massage it into your scalp to create a lather.
